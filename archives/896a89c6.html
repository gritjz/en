Preface

There are a lot of various articles to introduce the way to add background music to Hexo blog and they are mostly working well inside the post. Once you switch the webpage, the music play will be shutdown with page refresh, which is annoying.

In this post, I will introduce how to add a music player for play global background music with APlayer and MetingJS plugin.

This post is based on

Hexo NexT 7.7.2 release.
